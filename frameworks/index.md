## All Frameworks

### Growth
*Frameworks for acquisition, retention, activation, and sustainable growth*

- [Emerging Channels Framework](growth/emerging-channels-framework.md) - A three-ingredient assessment for deciding when and how to invest in new acquisition channels
- [Explore and Exploit for Growth](growth/explore-exploit-growth.md) - Oscillate between finding winning insights and expanding them across your product
- [Freemium Value Sampling](growth/freemium-value-sampling.md) - Show free users a taste of premium features interspersed with free ones to demonstrate full product value
- [Gamification Three Pillars](growth/gamification-three-pillars.md) - Structure engagement through core loop, metagame, and profile—the three essential layers of habit formation
- [Growth Competency Model](growth/growth-competency-model.md) - A framework for hiring, evaluating, and developing growth talent across four core competency areas
- [Marginal User Focus](growth/marginal-user-focus.md) - Find the user on the cusp of converting and design for their specific barriers
- [Opinionated Defaults](growth/opinionated-defaults.md) - Make it hard to do the wrong thing and easy to do the right thing—without eliminating choice
- [Positivity After Failure](growth/positivity-after-failure.md) - When users fail, show them their brilliance instead of their mistakes
- [Screenshot Tracking for Virality](growth/screenshot-tracking-virality.md) - Find organic viral moments by tracking where users screenshot, then amplify those moments

### Product Strategy
*Frameworks for roadmapping, prioritization, and product vision*

- [Adjacent Possible Navigation](product-strategy/adjacent-possible-navigation.md) - Combine a distant North Star with incremental steps within arm's reach
- [Editorial-Algorithm Hybrid](product-strategy/editorial-algorithm-hybrid.md) - Train algorithms on expert judgment, not just engagement metrics
- [Gardener vs Builder Mindset](product-strategy/gardener-vs-builder.md) - Look for things that can grow on their own, not just things you can manipulate
- [Solar System Portfolio Model](product-strategy/solar-system-portfolio.md) - Structure your product portfolio with a core 'sun' and satellite 'planets'

### Leadership
*Frameworks for managing people, coaching, and building culture*

- [Growth CMO Model](leadership/growth-cmo-model.md) - Modern marketing leadership for product-driven companies requires data, iteration, and experimentation DNA
- [Organizational Kayfabe](leadership/organizational-kayfabe.md) - Understand how organizations develop layers of collective self-deception
- [Slime Mold Strategy](leadership/slime-mold-strategy.md) - Embrace organizations as emergent swarms, not controllable machines
- [Wartime Product Management](leadership/wartime-product-management.md) - Structured approach to leading product during crisis and chaos

### Decision Making
*Frameworks for making better decisions under uncertainty*

- [Agency-Control Trade-off](decision-making/agency-control-tradeoff.md) - More AI autonomy means less human control—earn trust before increasing agency
- [Curiosity Loops](decision-making/curiosity-loops.md) - A structured method for gathering contextual advice from multiple trusted sources
- [Human-Algorithm Interface Design](decision-making/human-algorithm-interface.md) - Define what algorithms optimize vs. what humans decide—then design the interface between them
- [Inner vs Outer Scorecard](decision-making/inner-outer-scorecard.md) - Warren Buffett's framework for distinguishing internal fulfillment from external validation

### Communication
*Frameworks for storytelling, positioning, and strategic narrative*

- [Yes, And for Business](communication/yes-and-for-business.md) - Apply improv's foundational principle to build rapport, enable collaboration, and drive progress
- [Gift of Details](communication/gift-of-details.md) - Add specific, concrete details to create shared understanding and open up possibilities

### Team Building
*Frameworks for hiring, scaling teams, and organizational design*

- [Painter, Architect, Surgeon](team-building/painter-architect-surgeon.md) - Three archetypes for growth hires based on skill profile: generalist, strategist, or specialist
- [R&D Team Integration Model](team-building/rd-team-integration.md) - Three principles for setting up innovation teams that don't get rejected by the organization
- [Strategy Salons (Nerd Clubs)](team-building/strategy-salons.md) - Create opt-in idea labs that generate strategic insights through collaborative debate

### Execution
*Frameworks for shipping, operations, and process improvement*

- [Always Rules vs Sometimes Rules](execution/always-rules-vs-sometimes-rules.md) - Black-and-white rules are easier to keep than conditional ones
- [Cannonballs and Lead Bullets](execution/cannonballs-and-lead-bullets.md) - Balance your portfolio between big fundamental bets and small incremental experiments
- [Continuous Calibration, Continuous Development (CCCD)](execution/cccd-framework.md) - An iterative AI product lifecycle that builds trust through gradual autonomy increases
- [Problem-First Approach](execution/problem-first-approach.md) - Start with the problem, not the technology—especially when the technology is exciting

### Career
*Frameworks for personal growth, career transitions, and development*

- [Don't Be the Frog](career/dont-be-the-frog.md) - A heuristic for recognizing when gradual deterioration means it's time to leave
- [Eating Your Vegetables](career/eating-your-vegetables.md) - Build skills through repeated exposure, not natural affinity—10-12 tries before you know if you like something
- [Explore and Exploit](career/explore-and-exploit.md) - A growth mindset framework for navigating early career through intentional experimentation
- [Pain is the New Moat](career/pain-is-the-new-moat.md) - Competitive advantage comes from the painful learning journey others avoid
- [Eye of Sauron Positioning](career/eye-of-sauron-positioning.md) - Work adjacent to the CEO's top priority, not directly under it
- [PMF for Candidates](career/pmf-for-candidates.md) - A framework for evaluating job opportunities using three criteria: People, Mission, and Financials
- [Values Exercise](career/values-exercise.md) - A structured process to identify and stack-rank your personal values for better decisions
