### Career
*Frameworks for personal growth, career transitions, and development*

- [Values Exercise](career/values-exercise.md) - A structured process to identify and stack-rank your personal values for better decisions
- [Explore and Exploit](career/explore-and-exploit.md) - A growth mindset framework for navigating early career through intentional experimentation
- [Don't Be the Frog](career/dont-be-the-frog.md) - A heuristic for recognizing when gradual deterioration means it's time to leave
- [Eating Your Vegetables](career/eating-your-vegetables.md) - Build skills through repeated exposure, not natural affinity—10-12 tries before you know if you like something
- [PMF for Candidates](career/pmf-for-candidates.md) - A framework for evaluating job opportunities using three criteria: People, Mission, and Financials
